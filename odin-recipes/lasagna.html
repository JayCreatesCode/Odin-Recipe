Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that is perfect for family dinners. It features layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
